Welcome to our gallery.
An extensive collection of Chinese jade sculptures is currently available for purchase.
The majority of these draw inspiration from themes and motifs found in Taoism and Buddhism,
and others which also posses a unique aesthetic presence.

Pricing is determined based on assessments from experts with specialized knowledge of jade.
The appraisers are recognized for their integrity and provide impartial Fair Market Valuations.

Every piece of jade art comes with appraisal documentation,
including a detailed description and its Fair Market Value.
This will be available for your review prior to purchase and is included as part of shipping.

Our gallery operates without overhead expenses,
(i.e., no commercial space lease, no staff).

Only natural, untreated jade.
Guaranteed.
