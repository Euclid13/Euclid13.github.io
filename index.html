Welcome to our gallery.
A large collection of Chinese jade carvings is being offered for sale.
Most of the pieces are inspired by traditional iconography/symbolism (e.g., Taoism, Buddhism).

Since we are not qualified to do so, prices are based on the valuations of others who specialize in knowing jade.
The appraiser(s) are people who you will likely want to vet for yourself, and will find;
they have professional reputations and render unbiased valuations of each piece of jade art.

Each piece of jade art will have appraisal documentation (detailed description, Fair Market Value),
which you will see before purchase, and which is included with shipping.

Our gallery has no overhead costs
(i.e., no commercial space lease, no staff, we created this simple website).

Only natural, untreated jade.
Guaranteed.
